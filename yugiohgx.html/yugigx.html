Yu-Gi-Oh! GX

The Next Generation of Dueling Begins

Yu-Gi-Oh! GX is the second main spin-off series of the popular Yu-Gi-Oh! franchise. It follows the adventures of Jaden Yuki, a talented Duelist, as he attends Duel Academy, a prestigious school dedicated to training Duelists.

Main Characters

Jaden Yuki

The protagonist of the series, Jaden is a cheerful and highly skilled Duelist who uses the Elemental HERO deck.

Chazz Princeton

A rival Duelist with a strong personality. He starts as arrogant but grows into a loyal ally.

Alexis Rhodes

A talented Duelist and one of Jaden's closest friends, Alexis uses Cyber Girl monsters in her deck.

Storyline Overview

The series begins with Jaden Yuki enrolling in Duel Academy and quickly making a name for himself. As he navigates through friendships, rivalries, and intense duels, he discovers mysteries surrounding the academy, including the Seven Shadow Riders and ancient cards that threaten the world.
